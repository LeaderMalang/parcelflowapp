FAQs Package Delivery

How long does the delivery take?

Once your parcel arrives at our warehouse and is picked up by our courier forwarding systems, it depends on the service you have booked and the delivery time you have chosen. The transit time takes between 5-10 days depending on the parcel and your chosen service. Hence, it will depend if your parcel has been collected on the same day or the next day for it to arrive in the next 48 hours or additional days.

Can you deliver to post office?

It depends on the parcel couriers and the drop off points they offer. You can choose the nearest drop off points or ask them specifically to deliver at the preferred Post Office.

Can you change the delivery time?

You can change the delivery date of your consignment, organize to leave your parcel some place safe at the delivery address, have it delivered to your neighbor, drop it off at your nearby shop, or go to the gas station to lift it up.

What happens if you miss a delivery?

If you were somehow not available to receive the delivery package, you will receive an email or a note by the courier to inform you they tried to deliver. You can call or email them again to redeliver to your address or go yourself to collect your package.

Can I reschedule my parcel delivery?

Sometimes things can go wrong such as harsh weathers, accidents, heavy traffics, road closures etc. This can affect the courier’s ability to collect and deliver your parcel on time. In this case, we are very sorry for the inconvenience caused and would ask you to reschedule your parcel delivery according to your convenience.

How to know the correct size of your parcel?

It is imperative that you precisely gauge and measure your parcel for your invoice after it has been packaged to stay away from any extra charges. Dispatchers will re-measure and gauge your package on landing in the arranging terminal. All couriers maintain whatever authority is needed to add on extra charges for mistakenly gauged/estimated packages and we unquestionably wouldn't need that! This can be as much is £50.00 + VAT per package so it is critical. We would dependably encourage you to guarantee that the load and measurements are exact. To measure your package you can utilize either your kitchen or restroom scales, measuring tapes or even a FAQs ruler. You could likewise read our manual for measuring and estimating where you can discover tips on the most proficient method to get the correct estimations.
